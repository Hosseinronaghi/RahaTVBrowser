# RahaTVBrowser Privacy Policy

RahaTVBrowser does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, or an account service. Favorites, recent visits, settings, IPTV playlist references, and persisted folder permissions remain locally on the device. Web sites opened by the user may collect data under their own policies. Voice recognition is provided by the speech-recognition service installed on the device. The app does not include IPTV channels or copyrighted streams.
